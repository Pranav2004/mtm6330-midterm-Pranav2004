LAMBORGHINI ESPERIENZA Esperienza is the gateway to the world of Lamborghini. Dedicated to all enthusiasts of the House of the Raging Bull, this tailor-made program allows participants to experience the brand’s values with a packed itinerary which begins at the factory in Sant'Agata Bolognese, near Bologna in Italy. Part of the day is, indeed, dedicated to a visit to the new-look Lamborghini Museum, the prestigious boutique offering all the products from the Collezione Lamborghini, and the production lines to admire the creation of the Huracán and Aventador step by step. Esperienza then continues with the active part of the program. Participants set off from Sant'Agata Bolognese for the historic Autodromo di Imola race track, to take part in the exciting activities behind the wheel of vehicles from the Lamborghini Accademia fleet, supported by instructors / racing drivers from Lamborghini Squadra Corse. Exercises in specially equipped areas and track sessions will allow you to discover both your own limits and those of the Lamborghini super sports cars. Your nearest Lamborghini dealer can provide you with all necessary information on how to participate in the Esperienza

program.
